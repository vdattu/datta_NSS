About Us

About Netaji Sainik School, Kethanakonda Netaji Sainik School, Kethanakonda is a premier residential institution established to prepare young boys and girls for leadership roles in the Armed Forces and civilian life. Set in a tranquil campus on the outskirts of Kethanakonda, it blends a rigorous academic curriculum with robust physical training and character-building activities. Affiliation & Recognition: 1.Affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) 2.Recognized by the Government of Andhra Pradesh Our Mission: To instill discipline, courage, and a spirit of service, equipping cadets with the academic excellence and leadership skills needed for the National Defence Academy and beyond. Core Values: Integrity ● Duty ● Excellence ● Patriotism Key Facilities: 1.Modern classrooms with smart-board teaching 2.Well-equipped science and computer labs 3.Extensive sports grounds (football, hockey, athletics) 4.NCC and Scouts & Guides programs 5.Residential houses with dedicated mentors Admissions: Entry at Classes VI and IX is through the All India Sainik Schools Entrance Examination (AISSEE).
