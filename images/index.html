About Us

About Netaji Sainik School, Kethanakonda Netaji Sainik School, Kethanakonda is a premier residential institution established to prepare young boys and girls for leadership roles in the Armed Forces and civilian life. Set in a tranquil campus on the outskirts of Kethanakonda, it blends a rigorous academic curriculum with robust physical training and character-building activities.

Affiliation & Recognition: 1.Recognized by the Government of Andhra Pradesh 2.Proudly recognized by the Government of Andhra Pradesh, ensuring compliance with state educational standards and a commitment to quality education.

Our Mission: To instill discipline, courage, and a spirit of service, equipping cadets with the academic excellence and leadership skills needed for the National Defence Academy and beyond. ● Integrity,● Duty,● Excellence,● Patriotism

Key Facilities: 1.Modern classrooms with smart-board teaching 2.Well-equipped science and computer labs 3.Extensive sports grounds (Rifle Shooting,Horse riding,Football, Hockey, Athletics) 4.NCC and Scouts & Guides programs 5.Rifle Shooting Safe, professionally managed shooting practice to build focus and precision. 6.Horse riding under expert supervision Admissions: Entry at Classes VI and IX is through the All India Sainik Schools Entrance Examination (AISSEE).
